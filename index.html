Jihyeug Jang

I am currently a post-doctoral researcher at University of Geneva, working under the mentorship of Jehanne Dousse. I completed my Ph.D. at Sungkyunkwan University under the supervision of Jang Soo Kim. My research interests lie in algebraic combinatorics and enumerative combinatorics.
